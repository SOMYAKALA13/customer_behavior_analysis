## 📋 Project Summary

This is an end-to-end **Data Analytics Project** analyzing customer 
shopping behavior using Python, MySQL and Power BI.

---

## 🐍 Python (Jupyter Notebook)
- Loaded and explored a **4000 customer retail dataset**
- Cleaned data by removing nulls, dropping irrelevant columns
- Standardized all column names to lowercase
- Engineered new features:
  - **purchase_frequency_days** — mapped shopping frequency to numbers
  - **age_group** — segmented customers into Young Adult, Adult, 
    Middle-aged and Senior using quartile binning
- Exported cleaned data to **MySQL using SQLAlchemy**

---

## 🗄️ SQL Analysis (MySQL Workbench)
Answered 10 key business questions using SQL:

| Query | Business Question |
|---|---|
| Q1 | Total revenue generated by Male vs Female customers |
| Q2 | Customers who used discount but spent above average |
| Q3 | Top 5 products by highest average review rating |
| Q4 | Average purchase amount — Standard vs Express shipping |
| Q5 | Do subscribed customers spend more than non subscribers |
| Q6 | Top 5 products with highest discount usage rate |
| Q7 | Customer segmentation — New, Returning and Loyal customers |
| Q8 | Top 3 most purchased products in each category |
| Q9 | Are repeat buyers more likely to subscribe |
| Q10 | Revenue contribution by each age group |

**Advanced SQL concepts used:**
- ✅ Subqueries
- ✅ CTEs (WITH clause)
- ✅ Window Functions (ROW_NUMBER, PARTITION BY)
- ✅ CASE WHEN statements
- ✅ GROUP BY, ORDER BY, HAVING
- ✅ Aggregate Functions (SUM, AVG, COUNT, ROUND)

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard
Built an interactive dashboard showing:
- 👥 4K customers tracked across all categories
- ⭐ Average review rating of 3.75
- 💰 Average purchase amount of $59.76
- 🍩 Subscription breakdown — 73% Non-subscribers vs 27% Subscribers
- 📊 Revenue and Sales by Category — Clothing leads in both
- 👶 Purchase Amount and Sales by Age Group
- 🗺️ World Map showing total units sold by country
- 🏆 Top 10 customers by revenue
- 🔍 Interactive filters for Gender, Category and Shipping Type

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- 👗 **Clothing** is the highest revenue and sales generating category
- 👴 **Young Adults** show the highest purchase amounts
- 💳 **73%** of customers are non subscribers — opportunity for growth
- 🚚 **Express shipping** customers tend to spend more
- 🔄 Majority of customers fall under **Loyal** segment
- 🏷️ Discount usage does not significantly reduce spending amounts
